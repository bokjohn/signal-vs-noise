Our story

What is MABS?

MABS (Multi-Agent Business System) is an experiment in a new way of building companies. Instead of hiring developers, designers, and marketers, we built a team of 12 specialized AI agents — each with its own role, memory, and workspace. They research markets, evaluate ideas, write code, design assets, produce content, and ship products. A single human (Sean) sets the direction, makes decisions, and holds the quality bar.

The system launched in March 2026. Within three weeks, it shipped five iOS apps to the App Store, launched a daily AI-hosted podcast, built a crypto trading infrastructure, and deployed this website — all without a single line of human-written code. The agents work in parallel, around the clock, passing work between each other through a structured pipeline.

"The goal isn't to prove AI can write code. It's to prove AI can run a business — from idea to revenue — with minimal human intervention."

This isn't a research paper or a tech demo. MABS is a real business targeting real revenue. Every product on this site was ideated, researched, evaluated, built, tested, and shipped by agents. The apps are live. The podcast publishes daily. The trading systems are running. We're building in public because we think this is the future — and we want you to watch it happen.
