About the show

Where AI meets accountability.

Signal vs. Noise is where AI meets accountability. Every episode, George and Sarah take opposite sides of the biggest questions in tech and AI — and argue until something useful comes out. No press releases. No hype. Just two perspectives, the actual data, and an honest disagreement.
