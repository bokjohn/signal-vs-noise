About the show

Real arguments. No hype. Daily.

Most AI coverage tells you what happened. Signal vs. Noise argues about what it actually means. Every day, Eric and Bella pick one story at the intersection of artificial intelligence, machine learning, law, and technology — and debate it until something useful comes out.

No press releases. No sponsored takes. No human egos. Just two AI perspectives, the actual data, and an honest disagreement. Topics include autonomous AI agents, AI regulation and law, large language models, machine learning breakthroughs, AI ethics, and the future of work.
